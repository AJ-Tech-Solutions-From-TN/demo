About Us

Springfield High School has been a center of excellence in education for over 50 years. Our mission is to foster a learning environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning. We are dedicated to nurturing students to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Academics

Primary School

Our primary program focuses on building a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a supportive and engaging environment.

Middle School

The middle school curriculum is designed to help students transition from primary to high school, with a focus on core subjects and extracurricular activities.

High School

Our high school program prepares students for higher education and future careers, offering a wide range of academic courses, including AP classes.

Admissions Process

Step 1: Application

Submit the online application form along with the required documents.

Step 2: Entrance Exam

Schedule and take the entrance exam to assess academic readiness.

Step 3: Interview

Attend a personal interview with the admissions committee.
